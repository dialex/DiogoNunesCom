I challenged myself to take my camera with me on my walks around my neighborhood. My walks were becoming rather dull, always going through the same paths. And it's been 4 years since the last time I photographed.

[![](/blog/uploads/2021/08/2-zP7CByu-1024x1024.jpg)](https://www.diogonunes.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/2-zP7CByu.jpg)

This challenge was an excuse to practice photography again.

Also it forces me to pay more attention to my surroundings. There's beauty and uniqueness all around. We just need to notice it.

[![](/blog/uploads/2021/08/11-HxSYXGP-1024x1024.jpg)](https://www.diogonunes.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/11-HxSYXGP.jpg)

To challenge my skills I decided to use the 1:1 square format, a first for me.

[![](/blog/uploads/2021/08/4-AjkVdGr-1024x1024.jpg)](https://www.diogonunes.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/4-AjkVdGr.jpg)

Also I had this style concept of using a smaller inner white frame.

[![](/blog/uploads/2021/08/5-m1uKgf4.jpg)](https://www.diogonunes.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/5-m1uKgf4.jpg)

And sometimes make the main subject of the photo interact with the frame

[![](/blog/uploads/2021/08/6-K6w1ncr-1024x1024.jpg)](https://www.diogonunes.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/6-K6w1ncr.jpg)
