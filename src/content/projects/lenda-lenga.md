A friend shared a playlist of Polish techno-folk AI songs about local legends and asked if Portugal had anything similar. I said _not yet!_

Four months later I had composed and produced `11` songs (after generating over `1300` with AI), using a medieval style to retell Portuguese culture and myths.

I had no experience making music or writing lyrics, yet a record label published my music and I'm proud of what I was able to create.
