I was invited by OTIA to be interviewed. This was a first for me, so I accepted the challenge right away. OTIA is an online magazine that specializes on telling stories about professionals and their hobbies.

Since high school that hobbies played an important role in my routine. They ranged from photography, poetry, blogging, reading, open-source coding, gaming, origami, and more recently book coloring.

It ended up being a rather in depth interview, and forced me to find answers to questions I had never thought about.

It was an interesting experience for me. My interview focused on photography and blogging. [I hope you find it useful, like here.](https://otia.io/2019/05/03/diogo-nunes-software-tester-on-photography-blogging/)
