## Course overview

1. **Get motivated to learn photography:** I'll start by igniting your passion for photography. Discover the advantages of having photography as an hobby.
2. **Learn the concept of exposure:** Exposure is the foundation of photography, and understanding it is crucial to capturing photos that are not too dark or too bright. I'll guide you through the meaning of aperture, shutter speed, and ISO -- and their impact on your photo.
3. **Improve the framing of photos (rule of thirds):** Composition plays a vital role in creating visually appealing photographs. You'll learn the rule of thirds, a fundamental principle that guides the placement of elements within the frame. Discover how to create balanced and engaging compositions that draw the viewer's eye.
4. **Discover the different types of photographic gear:** Photography offers a wide range of tools and equipment to enhance your creative vision. We'll introduce you to various camera types and lenses, helping you understand their unique features and how they can enhance your work.
5. **Understand what can be achieved with post-editing (Photoshop):** Post-processing has become an integral part of modern photography. We'll explore the basics of post-editing and discover how to enhance your images, adjust exposure and color, and bring out the best in your photographs.
